Handsfree lets you keep your phone in your pocket while you handle calls on your Garmin watch or bike computer.

It's a companion to the application that you can install on your Garmin device from Connect IQ store: https://apps.garmin.com/apps/73107243-f322-4cf2-bb3d-78f2a4ee8920.

In combination with the watch app, it allows you, right on your watch:
• Initiate outgoing calls.
• Accept (or reject) incoming calls.
• See (and callback) your recent calls, missed calls.
• Control calls even if you initially started/accepted them on your phone.
• Mute/unmute the microphone.
• Automatically use the speakerphone when you're not wearing a headset.
• Control the volume of the call.
